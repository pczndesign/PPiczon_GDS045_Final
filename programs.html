Our Values

Mentor - Student

Our mentors at On One Studios are committed to the growth of their students because they are students, too! Each class is thought-provoking and personal to challenge dancers of all levels in different ways.

Commitment To Quality

Our hip-hop dance classes are a product of years of experience as a workshop-hosting organization. We’ve crafted our quality dance classes through rigorous testing and assessment so that we can offer our community a fulfilling and meaningful experience.

Variety of Classes

On One Studios offers a unique rotational schedule of dance classes featuring different teachers every week. The variety is great for students of all levels, and we have no doubt you will be able to find a class you love.

Hip Hop Choreography

Our Hip Hop Choreography program offers a comprehensive journey for dancers of all levels, fostering growth within a supportive and vibrant community.

Hip Hop Dance Choreo: This class provides a welcoming environment for individuals from all backgrounds to begin their dance journey. You'll learn foundational movements, basic techniques such as grooving, fundamentals, and rhythm, all while discovering the thriving San Jose dance community. Our dedicated mentors are committed to providing you with the essential tools and techniques to embark on your dance evolution. Come and experience the fundamentals of dance, the pure joy of movement, and the connection of a thriving dance community.

Intermediate Hip Hop Dance Choreo: Building upon the skills learned in the beginner level, Intermediate Hip Hop offers a space to challenge and expand your dance vocabulary. This class introduces more complex techniques, greater emphasis on fundamental practice, and more intricate choreography. We focus on developing concepts such as movement textures, integrating elements from other dance styles, and deepening your understanding of musicality. This level acts as a crucial bridge, preparing dancers for the more advanced routines. Our mentors are dedicated to challenging students, encouraging them to step outside their comfort zones, and refining the skills acquired through their previous dance experience. You'll have the opportunity to practice your technique, push beyond your comfort zone, and significantly expand your dance vocabulary.

Advanced Hip Hop Dance Choreo: Designed to challenge seasoned dancers, our Advanced Hip Hop class focuses on elevating your skills to the next level. Whether you're tackling intricate and extensive 8-count combinations or hyper-focusing on refining specific techniques, the goal is continuous growth and mastery. Learn from some of the best dancers the Bay Area has to offer, pushing your boundaries and breaking conventional approaches to movement. This class encourages you to challenge yourself beyond expectations, think creatively outside the box, and ultimately craft your own unique dance style.

K-Pop Covers

Unleash Your Inner K-Pop Star Are you ready to experience the magic of K-Pop? We offer K-pop classes that will not only let you learn the latest K-pop choreographies but also help you become a better dancer and performer. Our team of K-Pop class mentors is passionate about sharing their love of K-Pop with others, so they’ve put together an amazing K-Pop Class just for fans who want to learn how to dance like their idols. Whether it’s BTS EXO, GOT7, NCT, BLACKPINK, TWICE, or ITZY, we have something for everyone!

If you want to feel confident in front of crowds and perform on stage, then our K-Pop Cover Dance Class is perfect for you. You can even join us at one of our many events throughout the year where we bring out all the stops and show off what we’ve learned from these classes.

So come on down today and get started with us! It doesn’t matter if this is you are a beginner or if you already know some moves; there is something here for everyone.

Sensual Heels

Tap into your sensual side in our All Levels heels choreography class. Each week features a new instructor and unique choreography focused on building your movement foundation in heels. You'll learn balance, posture, graceful transitions, and explore footwork, body lines, floor work, musicality, and confident expression. Heels recommended (dance heels preferred), kneepads highly recommended. No heels? No problem! Join us to discover your inner power and the art of sensual movement.
