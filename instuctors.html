Hip-Hop Choreography

Dive into the exciting world of Openstyle choreography! This class is all about learning and embodying diverse movement qualities. We'll explore choreography that blends various urban dance styles like hip hop, popping, breaking, and even elements of jazz, lyrical, and contemporary.

The focus is on understanding musicality, storytelling through movement, and developing your ability to pick up and perform choreography with your own unique flair.This class focuses on learning, practicing, and executing choreography, drawing from hip hop, popping, breaking, jazz, lyrical, and contemporary styles to express stories and emotions.

Eric Pham

K-Pop Covers

Immerse yourself in the vibrant energy of K-pop! This class is dedicated to learning and performing the iconic dance routines from popular K-pop music videos. We'll break down the choreography step-by-step, focusing on capturing the sharp, dynamic, and often playful essence of K-pop dances. It's a fun and energetic way to improve your coordination, memory, and performance skills while dancing to your favorite tunes.

Paulina Vo Griffen

Sensual Heels

Unleash your inner confidence and explore the art of movement in heels. This class focuses on developing a strong foundation in dance technique while embracing a sensual and feminine style. We'll cover essential elements like balance, posture, and graceful transitions, ensuring healthy alignment and injury prevention. Learn to move with intention, express yourself fluidly, and discover the empowering feeling of dancing in heels.

Monica Chan

Hip-Hop Choreography

Get ready to groove and elevate your hip hop skills in this dynamic choreography class. You'll learn intricate and challenging hip hop sequences, focusing on foundational movements, musicality, and dynamic shifts. We'll delve into various hip hop subgenres, drawing inspiration from funk, soul, and street dance to enhance your understanding of rhythm, isolations, and overall body control.

This class is designed to push your boundaries and build your confidence as a hip hop dancer.
